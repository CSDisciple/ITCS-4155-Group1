About

Interactive Storyteller is a non-profit web-application
for the sole purpose of entertaining readers
by giving them control of the stories they read.


Each user-created and AI-created book features choices
leading to different outcomes.


We believe in creativity of the user,
but we also understand writer's block.


Our AI can write story for you.
