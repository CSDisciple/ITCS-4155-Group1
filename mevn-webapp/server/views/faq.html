Need Help? Checkout these most common questions...

What is Interactive Storyteller?
Interactive StoryTeller is a story-writting website. But, these stories are different from your traditional, linear stories. On Interactive Storyteller, the reader chooses the story. Each book written on Interactve Storyteller has any number of different choices within a book that may divert the story being read in another direction.
How do I create/write a book?
To create/write a new book a user must first have an account. If the user does not have an account, then an account must be created.
A user with an account must then be signed in so any book created can be associated with their account.

How much money do I make on my book?
Interavtvive Storyteller is a non-profit application for the enetertainment of the readers/users. Once content is published to our website, it becomes property of Interactive Storyteller, though to a specific user. The user agrees to this when using Interactive Storyteller.
Who is this website for?
Interactive Storyteller is for those who desire more freedom of choice in their literature. Users of this site seek a less linear approach to reading and writting stories. This breaks away from traditional stories that follow through to the written end. Interactive Storyteller changes this in that there can be many ends. This may be strongly popular in groups who enjoy fanfiction, speculation, and adventure.
Can I save my book to my computer?
